# Building-directory photos

Exterior photos for the condo-directory cards and `/building/<slug>` pages, for
buildings Auguste has NOT personally sold in (the sold-in buildings reuse his own
listing photos from `assets/sold/`). Each file here must be a free-licensed image
that is safe to publish on a commercial site. The `img:` field in `BUILDINGS`
(`functions/_data.js`) points at `buildings/<file>`.

Licenses accepted: Public Domain, CC0, CC-BY, CC-BY-SA. NOT accepted on a commercial
real-estate site: anything all-rights-reserved, unstated, CC **-NC** (noncommercial)
or **-ND** (noderivatives).
