# California Law & Business Exam — Study Guide Sources

Bibliography and source-of-record for [`law-business.data.js`](./law-business.data.js).
Every factual figure in the study guide should trace to a primary source below.

**Verification pass:** July 2026, against live primary sources (leginfo, CSLB, DIR/Cal-OSHA
Title 8, EDD, IRS, SSA). Statutory dollar figures and deadlines change — re-verify the
"drifty" items (marked ⚠️) before each new client build or at least annually.

> **Sourcing rule:** primary statute/regulation text and official agency pages are
> authoritative. Third-party prep sites (listed at the bottom) are for *practice questions
> only* — never cite them for a figure, weight, or deadline.

**Note on exam facts not published by CSLB:** the **question count (~115)** and a specific
**passing percentage (~low-70s%)** are *not* stated in any official CSLB source — they appear
only on third-party prep sites. CSLB confirms only: closed-book, 4 choices/one BEST answer,
calculator provided, "no penalty for guessing" (stated verbatim), and 3.5-hour limit. Treat
question count and pass score as unofficial estimates.
